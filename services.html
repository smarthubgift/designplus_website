Our services.

Staircase Design

Structural and aesthetic staircase detailing — straight-flight, spiral, floating and cantilevered — fully dimensioned with rise/going calculations, balustrade detail and fabrication-ready output.

Structural Steel Detailing

Connection design, member detailing and bolt schedules for structural steel frames — produced to a standard your fabricator can cut and weld from directly.

03

Tekla Structures Modelling

Fully coordinated 3D structural models used for clash detection against services and architecture, accurate quantity take-offs, and confident site execution.

04

Structural Drawings

Foundation plans, slab and beam layouts, column schedules and reinforcement detailing prepared to engineer specification and ready for approval.

05

Fabrication Drawings

Piece-mark level shop-floor drawings with cut lists, welding symbols and assembly sequencing so nothing is left to interpretation on the shop floor.

06

Site Installation

Setting-out drawings, erection sequences and on-site coordination support to keep installation aligned with the approved drawing set.

07

Shop Drawings

Detailed manufacturing drawings for joinery, steelwork and custom fittings, translating architectural intent into buildable component detail.

08

Architectural Draughting

Complete drawing sets — plans, elevations, sections and site plans — carried from concept sketch through to council submission and approval.

09

Council Submission Sets

Drawing packages formatted and annotated to meet municipal building-control requirements, reducing back-and-forth at plan approval stage.

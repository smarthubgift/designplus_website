Our Story

A studio for getting it right the first time.

DESIGNPLUS was founded on a simple frustration: too many good builds are let down by drawings that were rushed, under-dimensioned, or never checked against site reality. We set out to be the draughting partner engineers, builders and homeowners could hand a sketch to and trust the output completely.

Today we work across residential, commercial and structural steel projects throughout KwaZulu-Natal — carrying every drawing from first concept through to a set a fabricator or municipal office can act on without a second call.
